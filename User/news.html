Drive-thru

In 1975, in the US, KFC was the first restaurant to serve the drive-thru model, buying food without having to park, busy customers just pulled up to the sales window, ordered food and received the goods right there. KFC Vietnam will be the first fast food restaurant to bring this model to Vietnam. Suitable for the lifestyle of local people, facilities as well as the efficiency of the model: drive-thru will bring you hot, fresh food in the blink of an eye.
